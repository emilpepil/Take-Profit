How it works

Set a target once. Cinch handles the rest.

  1. 1
    Connect & claim

    Connect MetaMask on Monad Testnet, claim a demo bundle of USDm and test tokens.

  2. 2
    Set your target

    Create one or more take-profit rules per asset, with slippage protection.

  3. 3
    Keeper watches the price

    A server-side keeper checks the price every 10 seconds — you don't have to.

  4. 4
    Get paid automatically

    When the target is hit, the keeper executes the sale and USDm lands back in your wallet, with a Telegram confirmation.

Take Profit demo

Run the demo in 7 steps

Everything below runs on Monad Testnet. Testnet tokens have no real value.

  1. 1
    Connect MetaMaskSwitch to Monad Testnet and connect the wallet you will use for the demo.
  2. 2
    Get Testnet MONMON pays network fees. Take Profit does not distribute MON.
    Open Faucet ↗
  3. 3
    Claim demo tokensUse the in-app Demo Faucet to receive test USDm and tradable demo assets. One claim is available every 4 hours.
  4. 4
    Connect TelegramOpen the Take Profit bot and link it to this wallet to receive execution notifications.
  5. 5
    Create take-profit levelsChoose an asset, amount, target price and slippage. You can create multiple independent levels.
  6. 6
    Move the demo priceUse Demo market controls to swap USDm for the asset and trigger the target price.
  7. 7
    Confirm profitThe keeper executes eligible rules, USDm returns to MetaMask, and the result appears in Execution history and Telegram.
