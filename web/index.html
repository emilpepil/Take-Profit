Move demo prices

Swap USDm for an asset to raise its USDm price, or sell the asset for USDm to lower it. Quotes include the 0.30% pool fee and use 1% slippage protection.

Deploy PolicyVault

Each vault uses your connected wallet as owner and keeper. Deployment creates only the vault; funding and policy execution are separate steps.

Fund PolicyVault

Approvals are exact, not unlimited. Funding each vault may require up to three MetaMask confirmations.

Execute Policy

Manual confirmation only. The button unlocks only when the current price reaches a configured policy threshold and the vault has the required balance.

Assign test keeper

The keeper can only execute existing policy rules. It cannot withdraw funds or change policy settings.

Transfer vault ownership to Safe

Safe 2-of-3 becomes the administrator. Keeper stays unchanged. Each transfer requires owner confirmation in MetaMask and cannot be undone without Safe approval.
